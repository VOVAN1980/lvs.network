Living Value System

LVS is a new decentralized system where value is secured by trust, protection and regeneration – not by blocks, mining or gas fees.

Value that lives.

Already implemented: browser simulation up to 1200 nodes, trust dynamics, rejections and self-healing behaviour – even on a phone.

LVS turns trust and a protected minimum balance into the core of a new financial layer.

Why LVS is different

LVS replaces heavy blockchain consensus with a light-weight, trust-driven and self-healing architecture. No blocks, no mining – but the network still remembers, protects and recovers.

VU – Value Units

Pure value without gas and blocks

VU are the basic units of value in LVS. They move instantly between nodes without paying gas fees or waiting for the next block.

TC – Trust Credits

Trust becomes a measurable asset

Every useful action in the network increases Trust Credits. TC amplify the impact of honest nodes and limit the influence of malicious ones.

Vault Guard

A protected minimum that never burns

Vault Guard makes it impossible to lose everything in a single mistake. A protected minimum of VU is always reserved for recovery and future operations.

Regenerative consensus

The network heals itself

Cross-Audit and regenerative consensus allow LVS to detect inconsistent behaviour, reject bad transactions and restore coherence of the network without hard forks.
