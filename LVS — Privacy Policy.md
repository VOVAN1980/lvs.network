LVS — Privacy Policy
Effective date: 2025-01-01
Last updated: 2025-01-01
________________________________________
1. Introduction
LVS (Living Value System) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.
This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have under applicable laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
This policy applies to:
•	lvs.network
•	LVS demo pages (including browser-node testnet visualizer)
•	Any related publicly accessible web resources operated by LVS.
LVS does not collect personal data unless explicitly provided by the user.
________________________________________
2. Data We Do Not Collect
LVS does NOT collect, store, or process:
•	Personal identification data (name, email, phone, address)
•	Wallet private keys or secret phrases
•	Authentication data
•	Payment information
•	Biometric data
•	Sensitive personal data of any type
We do NOT run advertising tracking systems, fingerprinting, or cross-site profiling.
________________________________________
3. Data We Collect Automatically
For the purpose of maintaining website stability and security, we may collect minimal technical data:
•	Browser type and version
•	Device type
•	Operating system
•	Approximate IP address (anonymized or truncated)
•	Basic server logs (errors, access requests, performance metrics)
This information:
•	is not used to identify any user,
•	is kept for short-term diagnostic purposes,
•	and is automatically deleted according to server policies.
________________________________________
4. Cookies & Local Storage
LVS only uses:
•	Essential cookies required to serve the website
•	LocalStorage for UI preferences (for example, theme settings)
We do NOT use:
•	Tracking cookies
•	Advertising cookies
•	Analytics cookies (Google Analytics, Pixel, etc.)
Users may disable cookies in their browser without affecting core functionality.
________________________________________
5. Web3 / Testnet Interactions
If you interact with LVS demo tools or the browser testnet node:
•	Only public node identifiers are processed
•	No wallet keys, no personal accounts, and no sensitive data are used
•	Browser-node drift/SDM messages stay within the LVS testnet environment
•	Data is used solely for visualization and research of the LVS protocol
All interactions are technical and do not reveal personal information.
________________________________________
6. How We Use Technical Data
Technical data may be used to:
•	Maintain website functionality and performance
•	Diagnose errors
•	Protect against cyberattacks
•	Improve stability of the LVS demo and gateway infrastructure
We do not sell, share, trade, or monetize any data.
________________________________________
7. Third-Party Services
LVS does not integrate external analytics or advertising systems.
Some assets (fonts, scripts, styles) may be loaded from secure CDNs:
•	jsDelivr
•	Cloudflare CDN
•	GitHub Pages CDN
These services may collect minimal, non-personal server logs.
________________________________________
8. GDPR Rights
Because LVS operates in the European Union, all users have GDPR rights:
•	Right to access
•	Right to rectification
•	Right to erasure
•	Right to restrict processing
•	Right to object
•	Right to data portability
Since LVS does not store personal data, most of these rights apply automatically.
If you have questions, contact us.
________________________________________
9. Data Retention
We keep technical logs only as long as necessary for:
•	diagnosing server issues
•	detecting abuse
•	complying with operational requirements
Typically logs are deleted within 7–30 days.
________________________________________
10. Children’s Privacy
LVS does not target individuals under 16 years of age and does not collect data from minors.
